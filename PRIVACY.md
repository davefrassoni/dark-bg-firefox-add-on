# Privacy Policy for Dark Background Anti-Flash

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Dark Background Anti-Flash does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal
data, browsing history, website content, or analytics to the developer or to
third parties.

The extension processes the current page's URL and rendered background colors
locally in the browser only to decide whether and how to apply its visual
effects. This information is not sent off the device by the extension.

The extension stores user-selected visual preferences and user-entered
per-site rules with the browser's extension sync-storage API. The browser
vendor may synchronize those settings according to the user's browser account
and the vendor's own privacy terms. The developer does not operate a server and
does not receive those settings.

The extension contains no advertising, analytics, tracking, or remotely
executed code.

Questions about this policy can be submitted through the support contact shown
on the extension's store listing.
